Our complex is built to a very high specification with the flexible office space spanning the entire second and third floors of the building.

Canton Concourse comes equipped with everything you need. We have contemporary office furniture, high-speed Internet connectivity, a fully staffed reception, a state-of-the-art business lounge and cafeteria. With Canton Concourse you can begin working immediately.

Office Amenities

  • Parking Lot: A protected parking lot that can take up to 35 cars.
  • Luxury Lounge: A lounge comprising a 42″ panel TV with satellite/cable television and gaming facilities.
  • Kitchenette
  • One Client Elevator
  • Stand-By Generator

Office Services

  • Telephone Answering: A dedicated phone number and professional answering service.
  • Administrative and Secretarial Services: Our administrative and secretarial staff is available for filing, minute taking, dictation, typing or any other business service you require.
  • Network Access: Wireless and wired networking options, 24 hour IT maintenance and support.
  • Fax and Phone Lines: Multi-access telephone ports with local area telephone numbers.
  • Copiers and Printers: High speed printers, copiers and binding facilities.
  • Meeting and Conference Room Facilities: Users of our office space have access to all our meeting and conference room facilities.

Office Support

  • Receptionist: A professional receptionist who will receive your visitors and manage your personal telephone answering service.
  • On-Site Centre Manager: All staff queries and technical or operational problems will be addressed by the on-site center manager who is on call 24 hours a day.
  • On-Site Technical Manager
  • 24 Hour Security
