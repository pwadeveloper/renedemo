Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is your business center located where I need to do business?

Canton Concourse is located in the heart of Victoria Island, Lagos. We are of walking distance from The Palms shopping mall and are located within a few kilometers of all the major banks.

2. What if my office space needs change?

No problem. Since our business center typically has work stations solutions, we can accommodate your changing office space needs with ease. You can expand or shrink the size of your office space as your needs change. You only pay for the office space you need.

3. Can I choose my office configuration?

Canton Concourse offers various office configurations to suit your needs. Choose from a single-person office, a large executive office, a team office or an open floor plan office.

4. Do I need to supply my own office equipment?

You may choose to. However, Canton Concourse comes equipped with everything you need. We have contemporary office furniture and equipment meaning you can begin work immediately you move in.

5. Do you have administrative staff available at Canton Concourse?

Full-service office staff is always on hand to handle your day-to-day administrative duties or on an as-needed basis for those special projects. Unlike traditional leasing, with us there is no need to hire outside administrative staff to service your office.
