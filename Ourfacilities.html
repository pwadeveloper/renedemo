Our Facilities

Office Space

We offer office space that is adaptable to your business requirement, be it a small ‘one-person’ office or a fully staffed organization. Whether you choose to operate in Nigeria in the short or long term, we can tailor a solution that best complements your business needs. Our fully furnished and equipped offices come with an array of administrative and technical support services to meet all your business requirements.

Executive Offices

Suited to senior management, our fully furnished executive suites offer a superior level of sophistication. Extras include a meeting table, desktop printer, small safe, filing cabinet and direct telephone line.

Standard Offices

Our standard offices offer a favorable union of comfort and functionality and come equipped with office furniture and equipment, as well as administrative personnel.

Workstations

Should you prefer a less formal environment in which to work, we can partition any work area into cubicles or any arrangement you choose, to ensure that your employees can fulfill their tasks optimally. Workstations are available with flexi-time options on a daily or weekly basis, with full Internet access and administrative support.
